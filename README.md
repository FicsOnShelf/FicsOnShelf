# 📚​ Fics On Shelf
*FOS (Fics On Shelf) aims to centralize and automate the organization of fanfictions. Currently, readers consume stories scattered across various platforms (such as AO3, Wattpad, and others), often losing track of what they have read and, consequently, missing the opportunity to review them. To solve this problem, FOS acts as a virtual library based on automation, organization, and community. In short, it stands as the definitive tool for readers to document their literary journey in a fast, centralized, and social manner.*
